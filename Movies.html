Characters of the movie

The main characters of the movie are Lenny played by Adam Sandler, Eric played by Kevin James, Kurt played by Chris Rock, Marcus played by David Spade, and Rob played by Rob Schneider

  • Lenny is the main character of the movie, he is a family man, and he is the one who organizes the reunion with his friends
  • Eric is Lenny’s best friend, he is an easy going guy, and he is married and has two kids
  • Kurt is a loud and funny guy, he is married and has three kids
  • Marcus is a sarcastic and witty guy, he is married and has two kids
  • Rob is a goofy and clumsy guy, he is single and has no kids

All these characters can be seen in the movie poster, and they all have a unique personality that adds to the humor of the movie, and theyre all completely different people
