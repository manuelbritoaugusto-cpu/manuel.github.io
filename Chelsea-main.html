Chelsea

What is Chelsea

Chelsea Football Club is a professional football club based in London, England. Founded in 1905, the club has a rich history and is one of the most successful football clubs in England. Chelsea has won numerous domestic and international titles, including the English Premier League, FA Cup, League Cup, UEFA Champions League, and UEFA Europa League. The club's stadium is Stamford Bridge, located in Fulham, London. Chelsea is known as the "Blues" due to their blue home kit, and they’re a part of the "Big Six" of the Premier League alongside Manchester United, Manchester City, Liverpool, Arsenal, and Tottenham Hotspur (until their relegation).

Location

Chelsea is located in the Fulham area of London, England. The club’s stadium is called "Stamford Bridge" and it is situated in the southwest of London.
