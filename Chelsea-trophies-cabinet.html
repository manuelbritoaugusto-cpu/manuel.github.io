Chelsea's rivals

Chelsea has been playing against Arsenal; they’ve been playing against each other since 1907, and it's not uncommon to see a red card happen during the game. These games are usually called “The North West London Derbies”. While Tottenham is also in London, Chelsea doesn't consider them a rival since they both have a disliking to Arsenal.
